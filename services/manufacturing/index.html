Dedicated Clean Production

We maintain segregated production areas for carbon steel and stainless steel to prevent iron contamination. Our CNC laser cutting and bending operations utilize nitrogen gas to ensure oxide-free edges ready for sanitary welding.

Master Craftsmanship

Our certified welders specialize in TIG welding for hygienic piping and structural components. We achieve surface finishes of Ra < 0.4µm through electropolishing and mechanical polishing, meeting the highest bioprocessing requirements.

Validation Support

We understand the burden of validation (DQ/IQ/OQ). ALDR provides comprehensive documentation packages including material test reports (MTRs), and surface roughness certificates to support your qualification activities.

Global Logistics

From secure crating for sensitive equipment to managing incoterms for international shipping, we ensure your critical infrastructure arrives at your facility undamaged and ready for installation.
