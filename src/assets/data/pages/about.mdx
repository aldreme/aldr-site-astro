# Precision Engineering for Life Sciences

ALDR is a leader in high-grade stainless steel solutions designed specifically for the rigorous demands of **Bio-Pharmaceutical**, **Healthcare**, and **Advanced Manufacturing** environments.

Since 2007, we have been committed to delivering equipment that exceeds industry standards for hygiene, durability, and ergonomic design. Our products are engineered to support GMP compliance and maintain the integrity of critical controlled environments.

## Our Philosophy

### 1. Uncompromised Quality
We understand that in a cleanroom, every detail matters. Our products are crafted from premium **304 and 316L stainless steel**, ensuring resistance to corrosion, chemical exposure, and rigorous sterilization protocols.

### 2. GMP & ISO Compliance
Our design philosophy is rooted in regulatory compliance. Smooth welds, coved corners, and electropolished finishes eliminate microbial harborages, making our furniture and equipment fully compatible with **GMP Class A/B** environments.

### 3. Innovation in Manufacturing
Located in Suzhou, our state-of-the-art facility utilizes advanced **CNC machineries**, laser cutting, and robotic welding to achieve micron-level precision. This ensures that every workbench, cart, and storage solution forms a seamless part of your facility.

## Core Capabilities

- **Cleanroom Furniture:** Ergonomic workbenches, gowning benches, and pass-boxes.
- **Material Handling:** Heavy-duty transport carts, mobile storage, and lifting systems.
- **Process Equipment:** Mixing tanks, storage vessels, and custom fluid handling solutions.
- **OEM/ODM Services:** We partner with global engineering firms to design custom solutions tailored to specific facility layouts and workflows.

## Global Reach

ALDR serves a global clientele, providing reliable durable equipment to pharmaceutical giants, biotech startups, and research institutions worldwide. When you choose ALDR, you choose a partner dedicated to the safety and efficiency of your laboratory operations.
