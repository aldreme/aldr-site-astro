## The Vortex Problem

If you put a center-mounted stirrer in a round tank, the liquid just spins. It creates a vortex. The powder floats on top, unmixed.
*   **Bad Design**: A round bucket with a propeller.
*   **Good Design**: A tank with baffles.

## Baffles: The Chaos Generators

Baffles are vertical plates welded to the tank wall. They disrupt the laminar rotational flow, converting it into chaotic turbulence. This turbulence pulls the floating powder down into the impeller zone and forces the liquid to turnover top-to-bottom.
