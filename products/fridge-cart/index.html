Product Overview

Transform stationary equipment into mobile assets. Specifically engineered to support heavy laboratory refrigerators, freezers, and incubators, this Fridge Cart enables easy equipment relocation for cleaning or reconfiguration. The reinforced chassis creates a rigid platform, while precision laser-cut ventilation patterns on the side panels ensure the appliance's compressor receives adequate airflow to prevent overheating.
